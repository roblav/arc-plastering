About Us

Arc Plastering, established by Mark McRae, is a company that provides consistent quality and care to customers thoughout the North East.

Having trained in all aspects of internal plastering, Arc Plastering can provide a trustworthy service catering for all internal plastering needs. Arc Plastering has furthered it’s expertise to specialise in more demanding plastering methods such as artex covering, arched ceilings, skylight edging restoration, period stairwell ceiling repairs and more.

Arc plastering has been built solely on the referalls of satisfied customers and is registered with Rated People as well as being a member of the Gateshead registered trading scheme.

Whether you are looking for a small repair or a full house re-skim you can be confident that with Arc Plastering you will find a company that provides the highest standard of work.
